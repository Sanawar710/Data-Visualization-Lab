Data Preprocessing

The following steps describe how the data was cleaned and transformed to prepare it for visualization. This includes filtering, type conversion, and new variable creation.

Preprocessing Steps:

  • Removed rows with missing test scores: Filtered out rows where Math Score, Reading Score, or Writing Score were null/empty.
  • Filled numeric gaps: Replaced missing values in "Study Time per Week" and "Daily Sleep Duration" with the median value.
  • Handled categorical data: Ensured proper formatting of categorical columns like Gender, Race/Ethnicity, and School Type.
  • Derived column: Created "Average Score" by calculating the mean of Math, Reading, and Writing scores.
